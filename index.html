My Idol

Mahendra Singh Dhoni

M.S.Dhoni was born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi. He is a legendary figure in Indian cricket, renowned for his exceptional captaincy and wicket-keeping skills.

About

MS Dhoni is a famous Indian cricketer and one of the best captains in the world. Before becoming a cricketer, Dhoni worked as a ticket collector in Indian Railways. He made his ODI debut in 2004 and became popular for his powerful batting.

Achievements :

  • ICC T20 World Cup Winner (2007)
  • ICC Cricket World Cup Winner (2011)
  • ICC Champions Trophy Winner (2013)
  • Most Successful Indian Captain: India reached No. 1 Test ranking in 2009
  • IPL Success: Led CSK to titles (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021)

Records :

Most international matches as captain (over 200 ODIs and 60 Tests).
Fastest captain to 10,000 ODI runs.
Most wins as captain in international cricket.
First wicketkeeper to score 10,000+ ODI runs.
Fastest wicketkeeper-batsman to 6000 ODI runs.
Among highest six-hitters as a wicketkeeper.

Quotes
